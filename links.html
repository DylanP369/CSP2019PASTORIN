Recommended Links

A list of Dylan's recommended links to popular sites, ranging from games to media and much more, along with an age rating for younger internet users.


Zombs Royale

A 2D Battle Royale game, most similar to Fortnite with themed seasons. Recommended ages: 10+.



Surviv.io

Another 2D Battle Royale game, but more similar to games like PUBG. Best for use on slower computers or connections when Zombs Royale is not usable. Recommended ages: 10+.


Cool Math Games

A gaming site with mainly educational and fun games, from themes ranging from puzzles to action. Importantly, safe from your school's firewall. Appropriate for all ages.


YouTube

Everyone's favorite video sharing service. Content ranging from all ages to 18+.


Reddit

Reddit is a social site, open for news and discussions on certain topics. But most importantly for some, memes. Content ranging from all ages to 18+, most recommended for 12+.


Spotify

Spotify is a popular music streaming service, for free, but with ads. Content ranging from all ages to 18+, however, any explicit content is properly labeled.


SoundCloud

SoundCloud is another music streaming service where you can also post your own music for all to hear. Content ranging from 10+ to 18+, explicit content is not properly labeled. Parents, proceed with caution for your young ones if using.

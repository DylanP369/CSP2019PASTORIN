About Me

In New York, I went to the Immaculate Conception School in Kindergarten and the United Nations International School for 1st grade. In 2011, we moved to Portsmouth, RI, a few miles north of Newport.I went to the All Saints Academy for 3 years, and then in 2014, we moved to the town of Smithtown, NY, about 40 miles east of New York City. I went to RJO Intermediate School and WTR Middle School, and in 2017, I moved to San Diego, CA. I went to Bernardo Heights Middle School and then Rancho Bernardo High School. In 2022, when I graduate, I plan on pursuing a career in the military, computer science or a surgeon or doctor.
